Dungeons and Dragons

D&D wiki D&D Beyond Wizards of The Coast

    Dungeons and Dragons (aka DND or D&D) is a TTRPG (Table Top Role Playing Game) made by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. DND was made in 1974, it was published by Tactical Studies Rules. DND's current publisher is Wizards of The Coast.
    DND is played use seven diffent dice. They are 4 (d4), 6 (d6), 8 (d8), 10 (d10), 12 (d12), 20 (d20), and 100 sided die. Dice are rolled when the outcome of and action is uncertian and how you roll will be how well you perfom, the d20 is usually used for these kind of rolls. The other dice are used for damage.
    The Dungeon Master is the person who guides to players throught the world. They control and make desitions for all Non-Player Characters (NPCs). They will usually also be in charge of scheduling when they play.
